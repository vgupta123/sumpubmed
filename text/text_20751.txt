BACKGROUND
the family baculoviridae consists of viruses that contain circular dna genomes ranging in size from approximately  <dig> to  <dig> kbp. this family of viruses has only been shown to be pathogenic to arthropods particularly insects  <cit>  and have a very variable g+c content that ranges from 28% to  <dig> %  <cit> . the virions have a complex construction and consist of an envelope and a nucleocapsid. baculoviruses are divided into nucleopolyhedrovirus  and granulovirus  generally. based on phylogenetic analysis, baculoviruses can be classified into five major groups including gvs, group i and group ii npvs, a group of the dipteran viruses, culex nigripalpus npv  <cit>  and a group of the hymenopteran viruses  <cit> . npvs are mainly found in lepidoptera and other insects such as hymenoptera, diptera, coleoptera, thysanura and trichoptera. the virions contain either single or multiple nucleocapsids. all hymenopteran npvs virions contain single nucleocapsid. gvs have been found only in lepidoptera  <cit> .

previous reports demonstrated that there are  <dig> conserved genes present in all baculoviruses and  <dig> conserved genes present in all lepidopteran baculoviruses  <cit> , and a total of  <dig> genes are present in all lepidopteran npvs <cit> . to date, the complete genomes of  <dig> npvs and  <dig> gvs are published or available in genbank.  <dig> npv genomes have been published from autographa californica npv   <cit>  to neodiprion abietis npv   <cit> .  <dig> gv genomes published include xestia c-nigrum gv  <cit> , plutella xylostella gv  <cit> , cydia pomonella gv  <cit> , adoxophyes orana gv <cit> , cryptophlebia leucotreta gv  <cit>  and choristoneura occidentalis gv <cit> .

the chinese tussah silkworm , antheraea pernyi , is an insect that can spin silk cocoons and eats the leaves of oak trees. as an important economic insect, antheraea pernyi is commercially cultivated mainly in middle and northeastern china. it is also used as a source of food and for cosmetics. antheraea pernyi npv  can infect antheraea pernyi resulting in nuclear polyhedrosis that can potentially result in an outbreak of infectious disease. the average incidence rate of this disease reaches approximately 30% and occasionally reaches above 70%. the yield of cocoons can be decreased by 30% when anpenpv occurs. this viral disease has brought about huge economic losses to sericulturist in china.
