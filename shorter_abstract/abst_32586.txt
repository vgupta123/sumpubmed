our aim was to develop an infarct evolution classifier and a brain regions classifier in a rat model of focal ischemic stroke using spectraclassifier.
in the infarct evolution classifier, spectral features contributed by lactate + mobile lipids , total creatine  and mobile lipids  distinguished among non-infarcted parenchyma , acute phase of infarct  and subacute phase of infarct .
magnetic resonance spectroscopy  provides non-invasive information about the metabolic pattern of the brain parenchyma in vivo.
