metnet online is a web-based portal that provides access to a regulatory and metabolic plant pathway database.
metnet online enables pathways, interactions and entities to be browsed or searched by multiple categories such as sub cellular compartment, pathway ontology, and go term.
the study of plant biological pathways is advanced by easy access to integrated data sources.
today, various plant data sources are scattered throughout the web, making it increasingly complicated to build comprehensive datasets.
