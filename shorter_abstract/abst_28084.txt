the 2-cys peroxiredoxins are proteins involved in redox processes, they are part of the plant antioxidant defence and also act as chaperons.
in this study, the transcriptional activity of a small intergenic region  shared by at5g <dig> and at5g <dig> in arabidopsis thaliana was characterized.
