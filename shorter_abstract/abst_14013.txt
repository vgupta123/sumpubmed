readat integrates into both bioconductor and traditional r workflows, rendering it easy to make use of adat files.
readat is an r package for working with the somalogic adat file format.
somalogic’s somascan™ assay platform allows the analysis of the relative abundance of over  <dig> proteins directly from biological matrices such as blood plasma and serum.
