we have developed a new method for rna motif search that allows for a significant speedup of the search of complex motifs that include pseudoknots.
by approaching the element ordering problem in a principled way, we demonstrate more than 100-fold speedup of the search for complex motifs compared to previously published tools.
this approach uses a combination of sequence and structure constraints to uncover new distant homologs of known functional rnas.
in this paper, we study the problem of rna motif search in long genomic sequences.
we have designed a new algorithm for rna motif search and implemented a new motif search tool rnarobo.
