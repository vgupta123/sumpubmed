students of veterinary medicine should achieve basic professional competences required to practise their profession.
the present study used the guidelines of the “day-one skills” list of european association of establishments for veterinary education  to create an online questionnaire for assessing the skills acquired by students at the university of veterinary medicine hannover .
the results of this survey highlight that the opening of a clinical skills lab at the university of veterinary medicine hannover and its incorporation into the study programme are ideal tools to promote practical competences and foster the motivation to learn.
a main focus of veterinary education is on developing clinical skills.
furthermore, the students showed great willingness to use simulators and models in a clinical skills lab.
